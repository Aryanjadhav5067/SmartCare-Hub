Disease Guidance & Tips

Diabetes

  • Eat complex carbs (whole grains, vegetables) instead of refined sugars.
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes per day.
  • Monitor blood sugar regularly; consult your doctor for medication advice.
  • Best foods: Leafy greens, berries, lean proteins.
  • Limit: Sugary snacks, refined carbs, sweet drinks.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

  • Reduce salt; flavor with herbs/spices instead.
  • Eat more potassium-rich foods: bananas, spinach, oranges.
  • Physical activities like walking, cycling, swimming help regulate pressure.
  • Limit: Processed foods, red meat, excess alcohol.

Heart Disease Prevention

  • Prioritize omega-3 rich foods: salmon, flaxseeds, walnuts.
  • Swap saturated fats for healthy alternatives (olive oil, nuts, avocado).
  • Maintain an active lifestyle.
  • Limit: Deep-fried foods, high-sugar desserts, trans fats.
