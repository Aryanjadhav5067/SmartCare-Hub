Comprehensive Diseases Guide

Diabetes Mellitus

Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, blurred vision.

Management Tips:

  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in fiber and low in refined sugars.
  • Regular physical activity like walking, yoga or cycling.
  • Monitor your blood glucose regularly with medical advice.
  • Use medications or insulin as prescribed by your doctor.

Recommended Foods: Leafy greens, whole grains, nuts, berries.

Foods to Avoid: Sugary drinks, white bread, processed snacks.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Symptoms: Often no symptoms, headaches, dizziness, shortness of breath.

Management Tips:

  • Reduce salt/sodium intake.
  • Eat potassium-rich foods like bananas, spinach, oranges.
  • Exercise regularly and maintain healthy weight.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption.

Recommended Foods: Fruits, vegetables, whole grains.

Foods to Avoid: Processed foods, excess red meat.

Arthritis

Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, swelling, reduced range of motion.

Management Tips:

  • Maintain healthy body weight to reduce joint stress.
  • Low-impact exercises such as swimming, walking.
  • Use hot/cold therapy and pain relief as advised.
  • Consult a rheumatologist for medications like NSAIDs.

Recommended Foods: Omega-3 rich fish, nuts, olive oil.

Foods to Avoid: Excess sugar, processed foods, fried items.

Asthma

Symptoms: Wheezing, coughing, shortness of breath, chest tightness.

Management Tips:

  • Avoid allergens and irritants.
  • Take asthma medications/inhalers as prescribed.
  • Practice breathing exercises for better lung function.

Recommended Foods: Antioxidant-rich fruits, vegetables.

Foods to Avoid: Sulfites (in dried fruits, wine), processed foods.

Depression and Anxiety

Symptoms: Persistent sadness, loss of interest, nervousness, irritability.

Management Tips:

  • Consult a mental health professional.
  • Regular physical activity and meditation.
  • Maintain social connections and healthy sleep.

Recommended Foods: Fatty fish, nuts, seeds, dark chocolate.

Digestive Disorders (Acidity, Constipation)

Symptoms: Heartburn, bloating, irregular bowel movements.

Management Tips:

  • Eat smaller, frequent meals; avoid spicy, greasy foods.
  • Increase fiber intake and hydrate well.
  • Avoid heavy meals close to bedtime.

Skin Conditions (Acne, Eczema)

Symptoms: Redness, itching, pimples, dry patches.

Management Tips:

  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare products.
  • Maintain skin hydration and avoid irritants.
  • Consult dermatologist for topical/systemic treatment.

Common Cold and Flu

Symptoms: Cough, sore throat, fever, congestion.

Management Tips:

  • Rest adequately and stay hydrated.
  • Use over-the-counter medicines as needed.
  • Practice good hygiene to prevent spread.
