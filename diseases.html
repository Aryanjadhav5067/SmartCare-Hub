Comprehensive Disease Guide

Diabetes Mellitus

Symptoms: Frequent urination, increased thirst, fatigue.

Management Tips:

  • Low sugar, high fiber diet.
  • Regular exercise.
  • Blood sugar monitoring.
  • Medication and insulin as prescribed.

Recommended Foods: Leafy greens, berries, nuts, whole grains.

Foods to Avoid: Sugary drinks, white bread.

Hypertension

Symptoms: Headache, dizziness.

Management Tips:

  • Reduce salt intake, increase potassium-rich foods.
  • Regular physical activity.
  • Avoid alcohol and smoking.

Recommended Foods: Fruits and vegetables.

Foods to Avoid: Processed foods, saturated fats.

Arthritis

Symptoms: Joint pain and inflammation.

Management Tips:

  • Maintain healthy weight.
  • Low-impact exercise.
  • Medications as prescribed.

Recommended Foods: Omega-3 rich fish, nuts.

Foods to Avoid: Sugary and processed foods.
