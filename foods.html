Food Advantages & Disadvantages

Food Advantages Disadvantages
Apple Rich in fiber & vitamins, good for digestion Can quickly raise blood sugar for diabetics
Spinach High in iron, vitamin K, calcium Oxalates may reduce calcium absorption
Chicken High protein, low fat (if grilled/skinless) Fried/processed forms add unhealthy fat/salt
Rice Fast energy, gluten-free White rice is low-fiber, can affect sugar
Soda Quick energy boost, enjoyable taste High sugar, no nutrients, increases diabetes risk
