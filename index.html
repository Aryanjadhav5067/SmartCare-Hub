Comprehensive Disease Guide

Diabetes Mellitus

Symptoms: Frequent urination, thirst, fatigue, blurred vision.

Management Tips:

  • Eat a diet low in refined sugars and high in fiber.
  • Exercise daily for at least 30 minutes.
  • Monitor blood sugar levels carefully.
  • Take medication or insulin as prescribed.

Recommended Foods: Leafy greens, berries, nuts, whole grains.

Foods to Avoid: Sugary drinks, white bread, processed snacks.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Symptoms: Often asymptomatic; may cause headaches or dizziness.

Management Tips:

  • Reduce sodium intake.
  • Increase potassium-rich foods like bananas and spinach.
  • Regular exercise and weight control.
  • Avoid smoking and excess alcohol.

Recommended Foods: Fruits, vegetables, whole grains.

Foods to Avoid: Processed foods, high saturated fats.
